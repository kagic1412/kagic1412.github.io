Study Experience

Undergraduate Thesis

2023-2024

RESEARCH ON THE MECHANISM OF DIGITAL ECONOMY IMPACTING THE UPGRADING OF CHINA'S INDUSTRIAL STRUCTURE

  • Based on panel data from 278 Chinese prefecture-level cities (2012-2022), this study employs a fixed-effects model to examine how the digital economy influences industrial structure upgrading, highlighting the mediating effect of technological innovation.
  • Results indicate that the digital economy promotes industrial structure upgrading by stimulating technological innovation, a conclusion that holds after addressing endogeneity with instrumental variables(IV). Heterogeneity tests show this effect is stronger in coastal areas.

University Student Research and Innovation Grant

2022 - 2023

The Impact of Green Credit on Corporate Social Responsibility

  • Based on panel data from heavily polluting firms listed on China's A-share market (2015-2021), I employ a difference-in-differences (DID) approach to analyze the dynamic impact of green credit policy on corporate environmental responsibility.
  • Results show that green credit significantly motivates polluting firms to enhance their environmental efforts—an effect that remains robust after parallel trend tests and other checks. This study offers insights for improving green finance tools and supporting sustainable industrialization and ecological development.
